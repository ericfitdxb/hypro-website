Industrial Valves & Fittings for UAE Operations

Hypro Sarlu supplies a comprehensive range of industrial valves, pipe fittings, and flow control components to oil & gas, construction, water treatment, and process industries across the UAE. Our inventory covers gate valves, ball valves, butterfly valves, check valves, and globe valves in multiple materials and pressure ratings.

From small 1/2" threaded components to large DN600 flanged valves, we source to specification and deliver within tight project timelines. All components are available with relevant material certificates and pressure test documentation on request.
